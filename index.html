CESSY | MUSIC

Ces Sy is a singer, songwriter, and musician living in the Caribbean Island of Aruba. She performs versions of songs from the classics up to today's generation of music. Her genre is an integration of Alternative Rock, Pop Rock, and Contemporary Music. Over the years, she has developed a powerful vocal style which gives her performance a unique feel. That's passion in every performance! Her influences include Dolores O'riordan (The Cranberries), Alanis Morisette, and Gwen Stefani (No Doubt).
